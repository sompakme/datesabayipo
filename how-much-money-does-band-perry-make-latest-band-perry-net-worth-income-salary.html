Kimberly Perry (lead vocals, guitar, piano), Reid Perry (bass guitar, background vocals), and Neil Perry (lead vocals, guitar, piano) make up the American country music trio The Band Perry (mandolin, drums, accordion, background vocals). They signed with Republic Nashville in August 2009, and on October 12, 2010, their self-titled debut album was released. Pioneer, their second album, was released on April 2, 2013. “If I Die Young” was certified quadruple platinum in the United States and hit number one on the Billboard Hot Country Songs and Hot Adult Contemporary Tracks charts. Pioneer, the group’s second album, yielded two more number one singles, “Better Dig Two” and “DONE,” as well as the Top 10 successes “Don’t Let Me Be Lonely” and “Chainsaw.”

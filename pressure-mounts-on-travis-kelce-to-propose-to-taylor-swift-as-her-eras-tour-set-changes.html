Throughout Travis Kelce's football career, he has dealt with rumors he almost went broke, coverage of what he spends money on, and ex-girlfriends calling him out. This article will reveal why Travis Kelce is under a lot of pressure to propose to Taylor Swift. Then, the article will look at the changes that Swift made to her Eras tour shows in May 2024.

Taylor Swift Made Big Changes To Her Eras Tour In May 2024

From the moment that it was confirmed that Taylor Swift would begin touring again in March 2023, it was a gigantic deal for fans. In fact, Swift's Eras tour concert tickets were in such high demand that the presale resulted in a United States Senate hearing.

Given how passionate Swift's fans are about her, every concert that is a part of her Eras tour has garnered a lot of attention. That fact has meant that many of Swift's fans have learned about the small ways that she made any of her concerts on the tour different.

Then, in May 2024, Swift made so many notable changes to the format of her Eras tour concerts that even many of her casual fans found out. According to a May 2024 article from Teen Vogue, the Eras tour underwent a sweeping list of changes as it made its way to Europe.

While it may have surprised some observers to see that Swift's Eras tour change so much part way through, it actually makes perfect sense. After all, Teen Vogue's article explained that the show was changed to incorporate songs from Swfit's April 2024 album "The Tortured Poets Department".

For many rock bands, adding several new songs to a concert wouldn't necessitate that much work. For example, if Metallica decides to add songs they haven't played in years to a concert, they have to brush up on the tracks and maybe change some of the onscreen visuals.

In Swift's case, however, it seems pretty clear that adding songs from "The Tortured Poets Department" has caused her to revamp her concerts in many ways.

According to Teen Vogue's reporting, Swift has added new songs to her playlist and reordered many of the tracks she performed during earlier stops. Swift has also learned new dance moves and donned new costumes that match the songs she has added to her playlist.

Unfortunately, to make room for "The Tortured Poets Department", Swift had to condense parts of her earlier concerts. For example, Swift left songs like "The Archer!”, “Long Live”, “'Tis the Damn Season”, and “Tolerate It” out of her concerts. Swift also reportedly condensed the "Folklore" and "Evermore" sections of her earlier concerts into one.

According to Screen Rant, fans were especially disappointed that "Long Live" was cut from Swift's Eras tour playlist. This is because there was previously so much excitement that "Long Live" was part of the Disney+ movie version of The Eras tour since fans feared it was going to be cut.

According to a March 2024 article from Variety, the film version of Taylor Swift's Eras tour broke records on Disney+. The outlet reported that the film had 4.6 million views on Disney+ in just three days. That figure made the movie the most streamed music film in the history of Disney+.

Given all the changes Swift made to the Eras tour in May 2024, maybe it would make sense if Travis Kelce didn't propose, at least until things calm down.

Who Was Rapper Takeoff?

The purpose of this article is to provide a quick overview of Rapper Takeoff, as well as a brief outlook.

Migos rapper Takeoff was shot and killed at a bowling alley in Houston, Texas on Tuesday night.

A member of the Migos, Quavo, was playing dice with Kirshnik Khari Ball around 2:30 in the morning.

Takeoff was born June 18, 1994, and was most well-known for his membership in the hip-hop group Migos along with Quavo, his uncle, and Offset, his first cousin once removed.

In 2013, Migos became a household name following their viral hit “Versace,” which Drake remixed.

“Bad and Boujee” was the first single to reach number one in 2016.

Known for their significant influence on contemporary Southern trap, a popular and influential rap sub-genre, Coach K’s group is widely considered a major influence.

In 2015, they debuted at the top of the Billboard top albums shortlist with their debut album “Yung Rich Nation.”

In 2017, they signed with Motown and Capitol Records and released “Culture II,” which once again reached the top of the charts.

The trio completed its trilogy in 2021 with “Culture III.” They had just released a new music video for “Messy.” (AFP)

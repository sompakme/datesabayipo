J Lo Showed Us Her Rehearsal Dinner and Post-Wedding Brunch Dresses

Over the past two months, Jennifer Lopez has delivered a bridal wardrobe that proves exactly why she's a style icon—and why she deserves to be called one.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck made their Georgia wedding extra-special by pulling out all the wedding looks, including pre-and post-wedding looks.

In the latest edition of On the JLo, JLo gives all the special details of her epic three-day celebration. In addition to first looks at her rehearsal dinner and post-wedding brunch gowns, she wears three jaw-dropping bridal looks designed by Ralph Lauren.

For her rehearsal dinner, the "Waiting for Tonight" singer opted for a gold, backless halter gown with a plunging neckline. She paired the dress with champagne-toned platform heels and other gold accessories including a large bangle ring, shimmering clutch, and more.

In a newly released photo, she wears her honey-blonde waves loose and smokey eye shadow to match, as she stands in front of a wooden mirror in Affleck's Riceboro, Georgia home. The wedding look is included in a collage along with shots of the decor on their wedding weekend, including ornate chandeliers and lovely rose-complimented table settings.

Lopez wore a strapless blue-and-white striped dress, a flowing floor-length number featuring a thigh-high slit and paired with dramatic gold platform sandals. A beige fedora capped off her look, which was selected to complement the "rustic country-chic" decor that Lopez helped design for the event.

In a sweet black-and-white collage of brunch photos, Affleck's outfit for Sunday's event was captured in a sweet pic of the actor and his mother, Christopher Anne Boldt. She wore a linen vest and pants with a white button-down shirt.

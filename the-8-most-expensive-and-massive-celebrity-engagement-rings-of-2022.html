The 8 Most Expensive And Massive Celebrity Engagement Rings Of 2022

It is never a bad idea to make things official and take the next milestone in the relationship and these celebrities did not hesitate to ask the big question with the help of massive engagement rings. The second month of 2022 is not over yet but several celebrities have already started announcing their 2022 engagements. Numerous celebrities seal the deal with proposals here and there. These celebrities are now ready to take the big leap in their relationship and they want the world to know about it.

Nothing warms the hearts of the fans than seeing their idols getting engaged and sharing their celebrations of love to the world. With celebrities having tons of money, it is not surprising that the ring used in the engagement would included sparkling pricey diamonds and among the celebrities who used giant stones to declare their love to their partners are listed below.

Sasha Spielberg and Harry McNally

Sasha Spielberg has announced on her Instagram that she got engaged in January 2022. Steven Spielberg's daughter excitedly shared numerous pictures of her beautiful engagement ring from her partner of one year, Harry McNally. The American actress famous for her roles in films such as The Art of Getting By and Terminal has announced the lovely news through her Instagram account on January 5, 2022. The ring is features a round, brilliant-cut center diamond which is between three and four carats. It has a tapered baguette side stones which is set in a stunning white gold setting. The ring is estimated to be around $150,000.

Machine Gun Kelly and Megan Fox

Machine Gun Kelly proposed to Megan Fox last January 2022 using an unconventional statement piece. Megan Fox is always seen wearing the engagement ring that Machine Gun Kelly bought her. It is a two-piece ring which was designed by the musician himself with the help of Stephen Webster. The ring features a Colombian emerald and one diamond which is the couple’s birthstones. The engagement ring comes with two magnetic bands which represents the two halves of the same soul which forms a heart shape. According to his interviews, Kelly added some thorn in the design of the band so that it would hurt Megan if she takes it off. The said ring is estimated to be around $300,000 and $400,000.

Jodie Sweetin and her fiancé

Full House star Jodie Sweetin and her fiancé got engaged last January 2022 as well. Jodie Sweetin has showed of her gigantic engagement ring after being engaged via her Instagram. She posted the picture of the ring with the caption that she thinks she’s gonna like turning 40 years old. The ring features a marquise cut one-carat center stone with a diamond-shaped halo around the stone. The ring is estimated to be around $20,000.

Romee Strijd and longtime partner

The former Victoria Secret Angel Romee Strijd just got engaged to his boyfriend of twelve years on January 26, 2022. The Dutch model said yes to Laurens van Leeuwen when he proposed to her in Switzerland where they had a lovely picnin in the middle of snowy mountain. The couple then left the scene riding a helicopter on the way home. The ring is a sizeable classic engagement ring which features a round brilliant cut diamond with a two-tone setting containing three pavé diamonds and a centre stone. The ring is estimated to be around $400,000.

Lana Condor and fiancé

Lana Condor is among the celebrities to be engaged at the start of 2022. She got engaged last January 28, 2022 which was announced through her Instagram account with a caption saying that saying yes is the easiest decision she ever have to make. The actress added that she is the luckiest woman alive as she will be marrying the greatest man in the world. She shared that her fiancé has worked the Vietnamese company Paris Jewellers to create their custom ring. The ring features a three carat diamond which is estimated to be around $100,000.

Simone Biles and Jonathan Owens

Simone Biles revealed that Jonathan Owens has proposed to her with glittering diamond ring through her Instagram account. The couple got engaged on February 14, 2022 and Biles shared a photo of them holding hands. The football player proposed to her during their Valentine’s Day date and they shared the news on February 15 when Simone posted a picture with a caption that she cannot wait to spend forever with Owens and he’s everything she’s dreamed of. She also called Owens her fiancé in the post which confirmed the news. The ring features a large oval shaped diamond on top of a diamond encrusted band. The ring is estimated to be around $325,000.

Jennifer Holland and James Gunn

James Gunn proposed to Jennifer Holland on February 21, 2022 which he announced on his Instagram account. The Guardians of the Galaxy director refrained from giving too much details about their ring and their engagement with only using emojis as the caption to the picture of his now-fiancée wearing a gigantic engagement ring. The couple began dating way back 2015 and seven years later, they are ready to take the next big thing in their relationship. The ring is estimated to be around $150,000.

Simon Cowell and Lauren Silverman

The America's Got Talent judge Simon Cowell is among the celebrities who’s finally ready to take the next milestone in their life by proposing to his long time partner, Lauren Silverman. It was confirmed by Cowell’s representative that he was indeed engaged to Silverman when rumors about their engagement surfaced after Lauren was seen wearing an engagement ring. The couple first went public as a couple in 2013 after it was revealed that Lauren is pregnant with Simon’s baby. The two first met at the Barbados' Sandy Lane resort in 2004 while Lauren was in vacation with her then husband real estate mogul Andrew Silverman. Lauren stole the spotlight while running some errands in Malibu with her beautiful engagement ring which is estimated to be around $3.4 million.

Which Cast Members From 'Summer House' Are Friends In Real Life?

The cast of Summer House has changed a lot throughout the years, but their bonds have proven to be solid. Original cast members Lindsay Hubbard, Carl Radke, and Kyle Cooke have been doing share houses in the Hamptons for years. Kyle's now-wife Amanda Batula was added the following year along with Carl's ex and Lindsay's bestie Danielle Olivera. The biggest cast shake-up of all came when season three rolled around. The Giggly Squad girls, Paige DeSorbo and Hannah Berner changed the game when they showed up to the Hamptons.

Season six of Summer House is currently airing with Cooke, Hubbard, Radke, Batula, DeSorbo, Olivera, season four's addition Luke Gulbranson, as well as season five's Ciara Miller. Winter House star Andrea Denver traveled from Vermont to the Hamptons along with newbies Mya Allen and Alex Wach. It really is nice to appreciate the real-life friendships this crew has. Even though there is plenty of drama, at the end of the day, they are all just one big dysfunctional family.

6 Paige DeSorbo Was Amanda Batula's Bridesmaid

These lovely girls met in 2018 when DeSorbo joined the Summer House cast. The pair hit it off instantly, and it was no surprise when a teary-eyed Amanda asked Paige to be one of her bridesmaids. Fans have loved watching these two form a true friendship over the years. The entire cast attended Kyle and Amanda's wedding and got to share this special moment in their lives.

5 Carl Officiated Amanda And Kyle's Wedding

Carl Radke and Kyle Cooke are one of the Bravo sphere's favorite bromances. They met during season one of the show and have been attached at the hip ever since. Whether they are kissing in wigs or working on their monster of a brand together they are truly brothers for life. Radke says, “There’s no show on Bravo like our show. There’s no show on reality T.V. like ours.” He says even with the new people, they all hang out together." If being in business wasn't enough... Cooke asked his Radke to be his best man and even officiate his wedding.

4 The Loverboy Family

Kyle Cooke created the brand Loverboy, but he employed his wife and his best friend to advance their Hard Tea careers. The company began in 2018 and has become a hit in the canned cocktail industry. Hibiscus Pom, White Tea Peach, and Black Tea Lemon are the seltzer drinks of the summer. This Bravo-lebrity endorsement co-founded by Amanda Batula, Kyle Cooke, and Carl Radke has a foundation of love, loyalty, and friendship.

3 Lindsay And Danielle's Friendship

Lindsay Hubbard and Danielle Olivera have been loyal to one another since the day they met. Their Summer House friendship began early on in season two and has been going strong ever since. They have dealt with numerous breakups and makeups and have always stood by each other's side. In the midst of Lindsay's messy love triangle with Austen Kroll and Ciara Miller, Olivera decides to step in and stand up for her girl. During a heated moment... she battles it out with roommate Miller and even gets a glass of red wine thrown at her. That's a true friend right there.

2 Carl And Lindsay's Newfound Relationship

Sometimes timing is everything and for these two it couldn't be more true. Radke and Hubbard have been best friends for years and decided to take their friendship to the next level a few years back. Their relationship was explosive and toxic and didn't last longer than a few weeks. Carl Radke has opened up about his changed lifestyle and his decision to stay sober. Currently, love is finally in the air for these two Summer House guests! Amanda stated, "They'd been such good friends for so long. You know, Carl literally moved into the same building as Lindsay because he sees her as such a good friend, she's so supportive of him, and it's like, they are so close in so many ways. And I've always said that some of the most successful relationships are built on friendships first... So if Lindsay and Carl make this work, it's probably one of the most obvious relationships, and I'm just really happy to see them both so happy."

1 Mya Allen And Ciara Miller's Emotional Conversation

During a recent episode of Summer House, Mya Allen, and Ciara Miller opened up to the whole house. Allen started the emotional conversation by sharing how hard it has been to fit in with a predominately white group of friends. Miller in tears added, “Last summer I was super intimidated to come to the Hamptons when it was all … it’s predominantly white,” she said. “But there’s an automatic feeling of I might not be adequate enough to fit in when people don’t look like you.” Allen also in tears told Denver she was very hurt when he called her the wrong name at the beach. She added that it wasn’t malicious but, “I’ve been in the house for four weeks,” Allen said. “I’m Mya …” In one of the rawest conversations Summer House has ever aired, the entire group shared a big hug and promised to check in on each other more.

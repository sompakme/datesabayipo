How Much Money Does Christopher Ciccone Make? Latest Christopher Ciccone Net Worth Income Salary

Christopher Ciccone is an American artist, filmmaker, dancer, author, and interior and furniture designer who was born on November 22, 1960. He began his career as Madonna’s assistant, dresser, backup dancer, stylist, and artistic director, and he is Madonna’s younger brother. His first public appearance was in his sister’s debut music video, “Everybody,” in 1982. He participated in her 1984 “Lucky Star” video after taking part in a promo tour for her debut album. Madonna’s prior homes in New York City, Los Angeles, and Miami have all been decorated by him. He also directed her 1993 Girlie Show concert tour and constructed the staging for her 1990 Blond Ambition musical tour. Life with My Sister Madonna, Ciccone’s New York Times Bestselling memoir, debuted at #2 on the New York Times Best Seller List in 2008, and went on to reach #8 on Amazon.com’s bestselling list and #5 on Barnes & Noble’s bestseller list. Ciccone is a proud gay man.

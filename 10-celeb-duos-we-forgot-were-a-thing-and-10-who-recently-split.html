10 Celeb Duos We Forgot Were A Thing (And 10 Who Recently Split)

Every single day, there are new headlines about stars. Some focus on their work, like upcoming movies and TV shows. Some say they are just like us and feature photos of actors and actresses at the gas pump or the grocery store. And some of these news stories are all about relationships: Who got dumped? Who is engaged? Who is planning a wedding? Who was spotted? And so on...

If anyone out there has missed any of these recent updates, have no fear: This list, which is waiting down below for everyone, points out a couple of different categories that are loved by pop culture fans. First up, there are 10 stars listed out who are together and who we may have forgotten are together. Next up, there are 10 stars who recently split and broke up and divorced.

So get ready to catch up on all the latest dates and dumps!

21 Together: Zooey Deschanel And Jonathan Scott

Zooey Deschanel is an actress known for Elf, 500 Days of Summer and New Girl. From 2009 to 2012, she was married to Ben Gibbard of Death Cab for Cutie and The Postal Service. In 2015, she married a producer, Jacob Pechenik, but they separated this year. Now, Deschanel is dating Jonathan Scott, who is one of HGTV’s Property Brothers.

20 Split: Zayn Malik And Gigi Hadid

Model Gigi Hadid dated singer Cody Simpson for a couple of years before starting a relationship with Zayn Malik, who was part of One Direction. This started in 2015, then they broke up in 2018, then they got back together, and then they broke up again at the end of 2018. This year, rumors flew that the model was now with Tyler Cameron of The Bachelorette, but that relationship is over, too.

19 Together: Halsey And Evan Peters

Actor Evan Peters was with Emma Roberts for years. They were even engaged. However, that ended earlier this year. And singer Halsey dated rapper G-Eazy from 2017 to 2018, and then she got with musician Yungblud in November 2018. Now, however, Halsey and Peters are together.

18 Split: Bailee Madison And Alex Lange

Bailee Madison has appeared in Bridge to Terabithia, The Strangers: Prey at Night, Wizards of Waverly Place and Good Witch. In 2016, she started a relationship with Alex Lange, but they broke up at the beginning of this year. Current rumors say that this actress is now with Blake Richardson.

17 Together: Elle Fanning And Max Minghella

Elle Fanning has been in works such as Because of Winn-Dixie, Super 8 and Maleficent. Max Minghella has been in works such as The Social Network and The Handmaid's Tale. These two met while working on Teen Spirit and have been together for over a year.

16 Split: Khloé Kardashian And Tristan Thompson

15

Khloé Kardashian was with Tristan Thompson, a basketball player, and the two welcomed a daughter last year. It came out that Thompson cheated on her, though, so earlier this year, the two officially split. Before that, Kardashian married another basketball player, Lamar Odom, then filed for divorce in 2013, which was finalized in 2016.

14 Together: Anwar Hadid And Dua Lipa

Anwar Hadid is a model, and Dua Lipa is a singer. Last year, Hadid’s relationship with actress Nicola Peltz ended. After that (and after reports said that he was seen kissing Kendall Jenner), he and Dua Lipa were seen hanging out and holding hands, and this is now definitely a thing.

13 Split: Lady Gaga And Christian Carino

Up next are some details regarding one of the most famous singers out there… In 2015, Lady Gaga and Taylor Kinney, an actor and a model, got engaged. It broke off the next year. After that, Lady Gaga and Christian Carino, a talent agent, got engaged. It broke off earlier this year.

12 Together: Lily Allen And David Harbour

David Harbour, known for playing Jim Hopper on the Netflix series Stranger Things, is now said to be with singer Lily Allen. In the past, Allen dated Ed Simons of The Chemical Brothers then a builder and decorator named Sam Cooper; she and Cooper broke up in 2015, though.

11 Split: Bella Thorne And Tana Mongeau

Singer and actress Bella Thorne has dated Gregg Sulkin, Mod Sun, Benjamin Mascolo and internet star Tana Mongeau. These two were together from 2017 to early 2019, and in June 2019, she announced that she was engaged to a fellow internet star, Jake Paul. They were married the next month.

10 Together: Kirsten Dunst And Jesse Plemons

Kirsten Dunst is known for roles in Interview with the Vampire, Jumanji, Bring It On, Marie Antoinette and the Spider-Man trilogy. She was also in the TV show Fargo with Jesse Plemons, who played Todd in Breaking Bad. These two stars are engaged and have a son together.

9 Split: Demi Lovato And Henri Levy

Demi Lovato was with Joe Jonas for a couple of years and with actor Wilmer Valderrama for six years. After that split, she was with Luke Rockhold then Guilherme Vasconcelos, who are both MMA fighters. And after that, she was with designer Henri Levy, but this relationship has ended, as well.

8 Together: Penelope Cruz And Javier Bardem

Penelope Cruz was in Vanilla Sky, Gothika, Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides and Murder on the Orient Express. Javier Bardem was in No Country for Old Men, Skyfall and Mother. These two stars were both in Jamón Jamón in 1992, then they started dating in 2007, got married in 2010, and have two kids together.

7 Split: Emma Roberts And Evan Peters

Emma Roberts is known for roles in Unfabulous, Nancy Drew, We're the Millers, Scream Queens and American Horror Story. From 2007 to 2008, she was with actor Alex Pettyfer. In 2012, she started dating actor Evan Peters, and they even got engaged. However, it was reported this year that their relationship ended. Now, she is with actor Garrett Hedlund.

6 Together: Cameron Diaz And Benji Madden

Cameron Diaz is a very famous actress, and Benji Madden is a musician known for being in the band Good Charlotte. These two started dating in 2014, and early the next year, they decided to get married to one another, in their home in Beverly Hills.

5 Split: Liam Hemsworth And Miley Cyrus

From 2006 to 2007, Miley Cyrus was with Nick Jonas. After that, she had relationships with Justin Gaston, Josh Bowman, Avan Jogia, Mike WILL Made It, Dane Cook, Patrick Schwarzenegger, Jared Leto and Stella Maxwell. While making The Last Song, she met Liam Hemsworth, and they got married at the end of last year. In August 2019, she announced their separation. Then, she dated Kaitlynn Carter, and now, she is with Cody Simpson.

4 Together: Sarah Paulson And Holland Taylor

Sarah Paulson has appeared in 12 Years a Slave, New Year's Eve, Ocean's 8, Bird Box, Glass and American Horror Story, and since 2015, she has been with Holland Taylor, an actress known for roles in The Practice and Two and a Half Men.

3 Split: Fergie And Josh Duhamel

Fergie, a singer and an actress, started a relationship with Josh Duhamel, an actor from When in Rome, Life as We Know It, Love, Simon and the Transformers movies, in 2004. They got engaged in 2007. They got married in 2009. They had a son in 2013. And in 2017, they separated.

2 Together: Katy Perry And Orlando Bloom

Singer Katy Perry and actor Orlando Bloom started a relationship in 2016. They broke up for a bit, got back together, and they got engaged earlier this year. Before this, Bloom dated actress Kate Bosworth and model Miranda Kerr; he and Kerr even got married, had a son, then were divorced in 2013.

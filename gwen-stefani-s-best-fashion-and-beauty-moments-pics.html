Gwen Stefanis Best Fashion and Beauty Moments: Pics

Gwen Stefani is on a roll! In addition to wowing Us for the last two decades with her vocal prowess, the multi-hyphenate singer, songwriter, mom and designer never ceases to amaze Us with her fearless fashion and beauty sense. Clearly, we’re not alone because it was just announced that the artist will be receiving this year’s Fashion Icon Award at the 2019 People’s Choice Awards — happening Sunday, November 10.

From Old Hollywood glam to punk rock, the former Voice coach is always switching up her look — though a fierce crimson pout and icy blonde locks have become her signature. At the 2019 Met Gala, the star made an unforgettable impression on her fans, donning an intricate Moschino ensemble on the red carpet, complete with an oversized fur coat with a long train. Since then, she’s been serving up colorful street style, buzz-worthy looks on “The Voice” and stunning frocks for special events like the Monte Carlo Gala.

Fearne Cotton is an English radio and TV presenter who shot to fame after presenting few popular programs like the “Red Nose Day” telethon and “Top of the Pops”. Fearne was born in London to Mick and Lyn Cotton. She studied from the Haydon School and she is an animal lover who follows the pescetarian diet. She is a health freak, runs thrice a week for 5 kilometers and participates in several charity half marathons. Fearne has an estimated net worth of $6 million.

Fearne studied arts, a skill which she used for presenting the show “Draw Your Own Toons”. She has a great fetish for body art as well, and has said publicly in media once that she has eleven body tattoos. Her favorite among the eleven art forms is the one which is a fern leaf. It covers her right hip to the rib cage.

In her personal love life, she has always been linked with many celebrities. She was romantically linked to Ian Watkins, Peter Brame and Steve Jones. The skateboarder Jesse Jenkins had proposed her on her 29th birthday. However the engagement was soon called off, and the couple eventually separated after 9 months. Her current boyfriend is Jesse Wood, son of Rolling stones guitarist, Ronnie Wood. Wood and Fearne both had their first baby on February, 2013.

In the year 2007, she was the first female presenter of BBC’s Radio 1 show. She is currently hosting another show on Radio 1, and is also the captain of the show “Celebrity Juice”.

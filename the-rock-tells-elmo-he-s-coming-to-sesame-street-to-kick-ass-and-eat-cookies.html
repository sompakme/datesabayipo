The Rock Tells Elmo Hes Coming To Sesame Street To Kick Ass And Eat Cookies

Can someone tell how to get to Sesame Street?

There are simply some interactions on social media that no one can predict. The latest one between former WWE Superstar Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson and Elmo of Sesame Street fame is certainly one of them.

It began innocently enough as Elmo asked his followers on social media if anyone had ever seen a rock eat a cookie:

“Has anybody ever seen a rock eat a cookie? Elmo is just curious,” Elmo asked in a tweet.

This tweet caught the attention of The Rock, who responded to Elmo speaking about his deep love for cookies and claimed he would come to Sesame Street to kick ass and eat cookies:

“Yes, my friend. This Rock devours cookies. All kinds of cookies I’ll introduce you to #CheatMeals and it’ll change your life. Tell Cookie Monster to move it over, cuz I’m coming to Sesame Street to kick ass and eat cookies. And I’m almost all outta cookies. ~ Rock,” The Rock said, responding to Elmo.

Could we see The Rock appear on Sesame Street sometime in 2022? Only time will tell.

KSI and Tommy Fury will headline a star-studded Misfits Prime card on Saturday night.

The internet sensation will be hoping to defy the doubters and beat the Mancunian on his home turf.

Misfits Boxing co-founder KSI fought Joe Fournier on his last appearance in the ring, but the bout was deemed a no-contest after the YouTube star landed a controversial 'elbow' to originally claim a KO win.

Meanwhile, Tommy Fury became the first person to beat Jake Paul in Saudi Arabia back in February after claiming the win on points.

Also on the Prime card will see Logan Paul settle his longstanding internet feud with former MMA star Dillon Danis.

The two have been going back and forth on social media since the fight was announced, and they will finally go head-to-head on Saturday night in Manchester.

Who is on the KSI vs Tommy Fury undercard?

Alongside KSI vs Tommy Fury and Logan Paul vs Dillon Danis, the Prime card features a number of mouth-watering bouts.

Astrid Wett will face off with OnlyFans rival Alexia Grace once and for all after their fight in July was cancelled after Grace rugby tackled Wett through a table.

Misfit Boxing regulars Salt Papi, Swarmz, King Kenny and Anthony Taylor will all get the chance to showcase their boxing ability on the main stage in Manchester.

When is KSI vs Tommy Fury?

  • Fury's clash with KSI will take place this weekend on Saturday, October 14.
  • The fight will be held at the AO Arena in Manchester.
  • Ring-walks for the man event are expected to get going from around 11.30pm BST.
  • You can expect the main card to start at 7pm BST.

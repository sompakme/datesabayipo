A Stranger Shops For $100K At A Cartier Store With 'Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles' Star Josh Fl

Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles star Josh Flagg discovered fraudulent charges on his bank account for $100,000. The bank account of Josh Flagg was hit hard after a stranger took a shopping spree at Cartier for a hefty amount of $100,000. The incident was reported to the Customer Service Representative of American Express, but no suspect was surfaced yet as to who did it to Flagg.

On Wednesday, November 3, 2021, then 36 years old Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles star uploaded a video to his Instagram account where it was revealed that someone just used his American Express card to buy in the Cartier store. In the clip, it can be seen that Flagg was on the phone with the American Express customer service team to report the incident and get it resolved. When the customer service was asked how much had been charged to his card, the person on the line replied with '$104,175.64', and Flagg helplessly just joked about it and said that someone was having a great day. Flagg has said that someone spent about $100,000 on his American Express card at Cartier, and the phone call made to American Express customer service was recorded. It was a company policy to have it on record.

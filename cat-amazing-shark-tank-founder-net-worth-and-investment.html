About the Founder – Andrey Grigoryev

Andrey Grigoryev, the founder of Cat Amazing, has an interesting background that led him to create this innovative cat toy. Originally from Russia, Grigoryev moved to the United States at a young age and pursued a degree in history from NYU.

After completing his education, Grigoryev explored various career paths, including software instruction and social media marketing. However, his true passion and inspiration came from his love for cats.

Driven by his desire to provide cats with engaging and stimulating toys, Grigoryev founded Cat Amazing. Combining his creativity and expertise, he brought the Cat Amazing toy to market and revolutionized interactive play for cats around the world.

Education and Career

Andrey Grigoryev’s educational background in history from NYU provided him with a solid foundation in research and critical thinking. This knowledge has been invaluable in the development and refinement of Cat Amazing.

Founding Cat Amazing

In 2011, Andrey Grigoryev embarked on a creative endeavor that would later become the beloved brand known as Cat Amazing. Inspired by his own feline companion, Grigoryev set out to design interactive toys that would engage and entertain cats of all ages and breeds.

What started as a simple side project, crafting tiny mazes for his cat to explore, quickly gained traction and captured the attention of cat owners seeking innovative ways to stimulate their furry friends. The Cat Amazing creation quickly grew into a passion project that combined Grigoryev’s love for cats with his entrepreneurial spirit.

“I wanted to create something that would truly captivate cats and provide them with hours of entertainment. Seeing the joy it brought to my own cat inspired me to share this unique interactive experience with all cat lovers.”

In the early years, Cat Amazing generated modest revenue, bringing in approximately $20,000 annually from 2011 to 2017. However, fueled by the positive feedback and growing demand for his creations, Grigoryev made the bold decision to dedicate himself full-time to Cat Amazing in 2018.

With undivided attention and an unwavering commitment to quality, Cat Amazing’s sales skyrocketed, reaching over $300,000 within a year. The brand’s popularity continued to soar, and by 2021, it boasted an impressive annual revenue of approximately $550,000.

Grigoryev’s passion and determination to create engaging and captivating toys for cats laid the foundation for Cat Amazing’s remarkable success. Today, the brand continues to inspire and delight both cats and their owners.

Shark Tank Appearance

In 2022, Andrey Grigoryev had a groundbreaking opportunity to showcase Cat Amazing on the highly popular show Shark Tank. Seeking a $200,000 investment for a 10% equity stake in his company, Grigoryev presented his compelling investment pitch to the renowned panel of sharks.

“Cat Amazing provides a truly innovative and interactive toy for cats that stimulates their natural instincts and provides hours of entertainment,” Grigoryev passionately explained during his pitch.

The captivating product caught the attention of not only the sharks but also the viewers. As Grigoryev delivered his pitch, he received offers from multiple sharks, including Robert Herjavec, Kevin O’Leary, and Daymond John.

However, in a surprising turn of events, Andrey Grigoryev ultimately accepted an enticing deal with the esteemed shark and QVC queen, Lori Greiner. Greiner offered $200,000 for 22% equity in Cat Amazing, with an additional 5% going towards a cat shelter charity.

The partnership with Greiner has proven to be a significant milestone for Cat Amazing, positioning the company for even greater success in the pet toy industry. The exposure gained from the Shark Tank appearance has undoubtedly propelled the brand to new heights and opened doors to exciting opportunities.

The Impact of Cat Amazing on Shelter Cats

Cat Amazing is dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of shelter cats. In addition to supporting shelters, Cat Amazing also offers free toys to owners of rescued cats. By offering these toys, Cat Amazing hopes to reduce the shelter return rate by improving the cats’ social skills and overall behavior.

Cat Amazing’s commitment to shelter cats goes beyond just providing toys. The company also offers educational resources for cat owners on their website. These resources cover various topics such as cat behavior, training tips, and enriching activities to enhance the bond between owners and their feline companions. By educating cat owners, Cat Amazing aims to create a supportive and caring environment for shelter cats even after they find their forever homes.

Who Is The Richest Cast Member From Hocus Pocus Today?

Hocus Pocus wasn't a huge hit when it was first released in movie theaters during the summer of 1993, but over time it became a cult classic, thanks to airings of the film on Disney Channel and ABC Family. The film was directed by Kenny Ortega who went on to direct the immensely successful High School Musical movies. The film starred Sarah Jessica Parker from Sex and the City as well as Bette Midler and Kathy Najimy.

The movie also featured a cast of young actors, including Thora Birch, who went on to star in American Beauty in 1999. Other young stars in the film included Vinessa Shaw, Omri Katz, and Sean Murray who went on to star on the television series, NCIS. Several of the actors still have acting careers today, and fans may be curious to know who the richest cast member from Hocus Pocus is today. Let's find out.

10 Vinessa Shaw Is Worth $500 Thousand

Vinessa Shaw is known for playing the role of Allison in Hocus Pocus, the girl that the male lead falls in love with. She is still working as an actress but hasn't had a ton of major roles over the years. She played the role of Diedre Banks in the movie Side Effects and appeared as Kate McPherson in ten episodes of the series Ray Donovan.

9 Omri Katz Is Worth $1 Million

Omri Katz, who played the male lead, Max, in Hocus Pocus, quit acting in the early 2000s but has an estimated net worth of one million. He currently runs The Mary Danksters, which is a cannabis company, and is in a relationship with a woman named Michele Watters.

8 Stephanie Faracy Is Worth $1.5 Million

Stephanie Faracy played Max and Dani's mom in Hocus Pocus and has had roles in several movies over the years including Bad Teacher and Get Him to the Greek. She has also done a ton of recurring as well as guest appearances on various television shows, including Modern Family, Devious Maids, How to Get Away With Murder, and Uncoupled.

7 Tobias Jelinek Is Worth $2 Million

Tobias Jelinek played one of the guys who bullied Max in Hocus Pocus, named Jay. He has since gone on to have guest roles in many popular television series such as Stranger Things and Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

6 Doug Jones Is Worth $2 Million

Doug Jones, who portrays a corpse awoken from the dead named Billy in Hocus Pocus, and one of Winnifred's ex-boyfriends, is currently a series regular on Star Trek: Discovery. He also worked as a series regular for several seasons of the TV series, Falling Skies. He has also done a bunch of smaller movies over the years.

5 Thora Birch Is Worth $8 Million

Thora Birch starred as Dani in Hocus Pocus and went on to star in the Academy Award-winning film, American Beauty, which came out in 1999. Most recently, she starred in a podcast series called Overleaper, had a recurring role on The Walking Dead, and starred in a movie called 13 Minutes.

4 Sean Murray Is Worth $8 Million

Sean Murray, who played the role of Thackery in Hocus Pocus, who appears in the beginning of the film as well as the end of the movie, has gone on to star in the television series, NCIS, where he plays the role of Timothy McGee. He was also a series regular on the short-lived television series Harts of the West in 1994. It's also worth noting that he had a role in the Leonardo DiCaprio film, This Boy's Life.

3 Kathy Najimy Is Worth $8 Million

Kathy Najimy is famous for her role in Sister Act, playing the character of Mary. She is also famous for portraying the role of Mary Sanderson in Hocus Pocus. She has also had roles in films such as The Wedding Planner and Single All The Way.

2 Sarah Jessica Parker Is Worth $150 Million

Sarah Jessica Parker, who played Sarah Sanderson in Hocus Pocus, is most famous for her role as Carrie Bradshaw on Sex and the City, which spawned two movies as well as a sequel series, titled And Just Like That... Obviously a lot of her net worth comes from that franchise. She now also has her own line of footwear. The actress has also been on Broadway and appeared in three episodes of the television series, Glee. Parker also works as a producer on the Sex and the City franchise, including the sequel series.

1 Bette Midler Is Worth $250 Million

Bette Midler, who starred as Winnifred Sanderson in Hocus Pocus, is not surprisingly the richest cast member of Hocus Pocus today. She is also famous for her roles in The First Wive's Club and Beaches, but is also known for her career on Broadway, as she has appeared in 7 shows over the course of her career. She has also had a career as a singer, with many albums under her belt.

More dragons, more fire, more action – fans of the Game Of Thrones series were brought back to life after House Of The Dragon, which serves as a prequel to George R.R. Martins's Game Of Thrones, was first announced.

The series, set 200 years before the events of Game Of Thrones, chronicles the tumultuous history of House Targaryen.

According to Collider, House Of The Dragon is the fifth most expensive television production ever made, with a budget of over $20 million per episode.

This substantial investment paid off, as the series premiere was watched by over ten million viewers across linear channels and HBO Max on its first day, the biggest of its kind in HBO's history.

10 Emma D’Arcy (Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen)

Emma D'Arcy's Net Worth (Estimated): $500,000

Emma D'Arcy played the role of Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen, an ancestor of fan-favorite Daenerys (portrayed by Emilia Clarke in Game Of Thrones.)

D'Arcy is relatively new in the entertainment industry, having only recently made her breakthrough with House Of The Dragon.

Despite her novelty and limited experience, D'Arcy has already amassed a net worth of $500,000.

This impressive figure can be attributed to her involvement in a few high-profile projects, including the 2019 Amazon Prime Video series Truth Seekers and the 2019 BBC Three drama Wanderlust.

While her acting career is still in its early stages, D'Arcy is already making a name for herself in the industry.

Her role in House Of The Dragon catapulted her to stardom, and her image and net worth are likely to increase significantly in the coming years, mirroring Emilia Clarke's meteoric rise before her iconic role in Game Of Thrones.

9 Fabien Frankel (Ser Criston Cole)

Fabien Frankel's Net Worth (Estimated): $1 Million

Fabien Frankel's net worth stands at an estimated $1 million, a substantial sum for the rising star.

The English actor and model portrayed Ser Criston Cole, a skilled and hotheaded swordsman from the Dornish Marches tasked with protecting Princess Rhaenyra as her Kings guard knight.

The role has undoubtedly contributed to his financial success, as has Frankel's involvement in other notable projects, such as Last Christmas and The Serpent.

Frankel was cast as the lead Theo Sipowicz in a pilot for a potential NYPD Blue spin-off, further bolstering his wealth.

His anticipated recurring role in House Of The Dragon's second season could significantly boost his worth, potentially adding more zeros to his paycheck.

7 Olivia Cooke (Lady Alicent Hightower)

Olivia Cooke's Net Worth (Estimated): $2 Million

5 Eve Best (Princess Rhaenys Targaryen)

Eve Best's Net Worth (Estimated): $3 Million

Eve Best's extensive filmography and award collection are both strong proof of her talent.

Her on-screen performances have been recognized through numerous accolades, including an Olivier Award and two Tony Award nominations.

Best's breakout role in the critically acclaimed Showtime series Nurse Jackie brought her widespread recognition, and she has continued to impress audiences with her performances in various films and television shows, including The King's Speech, The Inspector Lynley Mysteries, and Waking the Dead.

Best plays Princess Rhaenys Targaryen, the wife of Lord Corlys and the cousin of King Viserys I Targaryen.

The English actress and director has a net worth of around $3 million. Her net worth reflects not only her memorable on-screen roles but also her directorial endeavors.

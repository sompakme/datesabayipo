AEW Revolution Announced, Takes Place Feb. 29, 2020

All Elite Wrestling’s next pay-per-view will take place on a leap day.

Called AEW Revolution, the event will take place on Saturday, February 29, 2020. It will see the company return to Chicago and will take place at the Wintrust Arena. Tickets will go on sale next Friday, Dec. 20. It will coincide with the C2E2 expo, which takes place from Feb. 28 through March 1.

“Revolution will be a huge pay-per-view event for AEW, featuring the biggest wrestling card we’ve ever presented, at the heart of a three-day destination event in partnership with C2E2,” said president Tony Khan. “The experience in the South Loop of Chicago will be unlike anything we’ve seen in wrestling, and it will be all day, all night and all weekend.”

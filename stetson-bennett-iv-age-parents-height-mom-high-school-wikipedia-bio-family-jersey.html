Stetson Fleming Bennett IV is an American football quarterback for the Georgia Bulldogs.

Stetson Fleming Bennett IV was born on October 28, 1998, in Blackshear, Georgia, the United States of America to parents, Stetson and Denise Bennett, who are both UGA graduates.

Stetson Fleming Bennett IV’s grandfather, Buddy Bennett, played quarterback at South Carolina. He played high school football at Pierce County High School and was a two-star quarterback coming out of high school.

Stetson Fleming Bennett IV committed to the University of Georgia where he played for the Georgia Bulldogs football team. He was a walk-on at Georgia in his freshman season. In 2018, he decided to transfer to Jones County Junior College.

Following his transfer, Stetson Fleming Bennett IV played in 12 games and threw for 16 touchdowns for 1,840 yards. He ended up returning to Georgia after accepting their scholarship and became their 2nd string QB.

Stetson Fleming Bennett IV is a recipient of the Dr Archie Sime and Bettye Rushton Family Scholarship, as well as the Irvin W. Wofford Football Scholarship.

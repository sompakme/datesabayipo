Abigail Spencer: What's In My Bag?

“I was the girl in middle school who carried all of her books because I might have the chance to study,” Abigail Spencer tells Us Weekly. Flash forward 20 years, and the star of Rectify (SundanceTV, premieres July 9, 10 p.m.) still uses a backpack — only now it’s crammed with travel essentials. “Sometimes, I’m on planes two to three times a week!” the actress says. The mom of Roman, 6 (with ex Andrew Pruett), briefs Us on her Tumi knapsack.

PHOTOS: Foxy Foursome

Saving Face

“I carry this EMK Placental Anti-Aging mask for when I fly. It’s totally sheer so nobody knows that you’re wearing it. I’ve noticed a huge difference.”

Sound Statement

“My ears are really small, so I wear Frends over-the-ear headphones. They look so cool. I’ll pull them around my neck and they look like a necklace!”

Arrival Revival

“Saje’s Travel Safe Kit has really helped me. It’s an aromatherapy set that has healing ointment, an eye mask, and digestive oil. It’s so compact, which is great since I live out of my bag.”

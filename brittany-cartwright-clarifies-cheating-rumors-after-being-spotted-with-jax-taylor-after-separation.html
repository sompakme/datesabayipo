Brittany Cartwright Clarifies Cheating Rumors After Being Spotted With Jax Taylor After Separation

Highlights

  • Brittany Cartwright denies there was cheating involved in her separation from Jax Taylor, stressing focus on son's well-being amid split.
  • Despite their separation, Brittany and Jax have seen together, handling domestic tasks amicably, hinting at potential reconciliation in the future.
  • While open to reconciliation, Brittany accepts the possibility of a permanent split, prioritizing their son's happiness and well-being in the process.

In February, Brittany Cartwright announced that she and Jax Taylor were taking a break from their marriage. The pair have been married for almost five years, and together for almost a decade, so of course this decision wasn't easy to make, but they weren't getting along, and they decided to take some time apart to make sure their son wasn't exposed to a toxic dynamic.

Ever since she announced it, the estranged couple have been bombarded with rumors and questions, and recently Brittany had enough and decided to address the most prominent one: if she left their house because Jax cheated on her.

Brittany Cartwright Denies Rumors That Jax Taylor Cheated On Her

There have been tons of rumors going around about Brittany Cartwright and Jax Taylor's separation, and they are frankly getting tired of them. The reasons why they split up are not for anyone but them to know, but Brittany Cartwright wants to make it clear that there was no cheating involved.

"The truth is, is that we have just been fighting and not on the same page for a while now and it was just becoming a negative space in our own house," she said about it. "I just don't want that for our son. I don't want him growing up thinking it's normal to live like that."

"Jax did not cheat on me, but we got into a very bad fight and I decided to pack up my stuff and I've been staying in an Airbnb since January 24," she added, revealing that they had been separated long before they shared it with the world. "The only reason that I felt like I had to announce was because the Instagram sleuths literally realized that I was staying at a different house and rumors started spinning going crazy everywhere."

This decision probably wasn't easy, especially considering that the couple share a young son, but it was ultimately the best for everyone involved.

Brittany And Jax Were Recently Spotted Together

Despite the separation, the estranged couple seem to be keeping things cordial. A few days ago, Jax Taylor was spotted at Brittany Cartwright's rental home, and although they kept their distance, they acted pretty domestic. She was carrying the mail and a pile of clothes, while he took out her trash bins for her.

It's important to note that the pair aren't necessarily headed toward divorce. In fact, when she announced the separation, Brittany said that they were taking some time apart. "I'm doing it for my mental space," she said, "and I feel very strong with my decision to get out for a while."

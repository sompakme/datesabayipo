Eve Torres Reveals Shes Tested Positive For COVID-19

Former WWE Divas Champion Eve Torres has revealed on Instagram that she has tested positive for COVID-19.

The winner of the 2007 WWE Divas Search, who would go on to become WWE Divas Champion, revealed on Instagram that she and her husband, Rener Gracie, have tested positive for the virus and unfortunately, Eve assumes that her young son, who has a fever, is also positive for COVID.

WrestleZone will continue to report any and all updates and we wish the Gracie family a very speedy recovery.

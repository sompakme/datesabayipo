Jennifer Lopex has been a household name since the '90s. The singer has built a $400 million empire and she makes approximately $40 million per year. Most of J.Lo's fortune comes from music, movies and also commercial partnerships with high-profile brands.

RELATED: 10 Crazy Expensive Things Mariah Carey Has Bought

It's no surprise that she isn't the type that looks at the price tag when she is shopping. J.Lo can afford the best life that money can buy. She spends part of her fortune on high-fashion brands, real state, parties, and traveling.

Are you curious to know how J.Lo uses her money? Keep scrolling and discover some of her most extravagant purchases!

Properties

Jennifer Lopez grew up in a two-story house in the Bronx. Most likely, back then she couldn't imagine that she would have multiple homes in Miami, Bel Air and New York, and they're all worth millions. In 2016, she bought a mansion in Bel Air for $28 million. The place has eight bedrooms, 13 bathrooms, a garden that resembles a jungle, a swimming pond, and even a bridge.

The singer loves enormous properties, which can be a problem sometimes. A few years ago, an intruder lived in her house for one week and people didn't notice it.

Beauty Treatments

Jennifer Lopez is known for her youthful looks and flawless skin. She is naturally beautiful, but she doesn't get it all from her good genes. Once a week, the singer spends $1.200 ($4.800 per month) in a human placenta facial mask. Specialists claim that the placenta is full of vitamins and powerful rejuvenescent since it helps skin regeneration. The mask is applied on the face, earlobes, and eyelids. It stimulates the production of collagen and elastin.

Although this sounds strange, other celebrities like Kim Kardashian are fans of the treatment as well.

Romantic Holidays

Paris is one of the best choices if you are looking for a romantic destination. In 2017, J.Lo and Alex Rodriguez traveled to Paris and other cities in France during the summer.

RELATED: Gossip Girl’s Entire Cast Net Worth

The couple is very much in love and shared many pictures of their holidays on social media. They also didn't mind when the paparazzi followed them on Louvre and Seine. After Paris, they went to the South of France and rented a yacht. It was their first romantic trip together and the couple didn't mind on spending over $350,000 on it.

Her Birthday Dress

It is impossible to ignore Jennifer Lopez when she enters a room. She is not only beautiful, but she also knows how to choose clothes that highlight her strong points. Lopez also loves to wear high-fashion brands like Versace and Dior.

For her 50th birthday, she chose a customized golden Versace dress. According to The Hollywood Reporter, a similar outfit from the brand cost $15,000, but the value of a customized version was not revealed. The price honestly seems like a bargain to J.Lo. She already wore an $82,000 outfit for a date with her boyfriend.

Body Insurance

Jennifer Lopez had her legs and butt insured. If something happened to those parts, she would receive $27 million, according to People. However, other sources claim she had a $300 million insurance. Although Lopez never confirmed the values, she sometimes talks about how her family always gave her compliments for her body.

RELATED:  10 Celebrities Who Have Their Body Parts Insured

It's definitely common for celebrities and athletes to have a body part insured. Famous faces like Cristiano Ronaldo and Julia Roberts have also done it.

Cars

It might be challenging to find a perfect present to give J.Lo. but her boyfriends seems to know her better than anyone else. Alex Rodriguez gave J.Lo a red Porsche 911 GTS convertible for her 50th birthday. "It came with a big gold bow on it and was delivered to their house this afternoon. The license plate says JLO," an insider told E! News.

The vehicle costs approximately $140,000. Unsurprisingly, Lopez has other luxury cars in her garage. She also has a BMW's 3 Series ($60,000), a Rolls Royce Ghost ($500,000), a Maybach 57 S ($500,000) and an Aston Martin ($210,000).

Personal chef

Having a personal chef available to prepare your meals and travel the world with you is a luxury that few people in the world can afford. Jennifer Lopez is one of them. She hired Ron Gelish, who it's fair to say charges a small fortune.

RELATED:  10 Most Expensive Restaurant Meals in The World

The chef used to have a restaurant in New York. He is very discreet and even during interviews, he doesn't give details about what it's like to work with Jennifer Lopez. But she does follow a strict diet. Other celebrities like Mariah Carey and Pierce Brosnan have their personal chefs.

Two Personal Trainers

Jennifer Lopez has a fantastic body and she also needs to be prepared when she is performing on stage. The singer has two personal trainers. One of them is Tracy Anderson, who is also a fitness entrepreneur. Among her clients, there are multiple A-list celebrities like Jennifer Aniston, Kim Kardashian, and Madonna.

Anderson shared many details about Jennifer Lopez training on her website. As Tracy Anderson told WWD, “She gives so much and she’s not one of these performers who think they’re just entitled to a good body and is jumping for all kinds of quick fixes. She’s very aware of finding her own formula and what works for her along the way.”

Weddings

This year, Jennifer Lopez will get married to Alex Rodriguez, and it'll be the fourth time that the singer walks down the aisle. She got married the first time in 1998, in a simple ceremony.

However, she went the extra mile when she walked in the aisle for the second time and had to spend lots of money to assure her privacy. As her ex-husband Cris Judd said in an interview with Us Weekly,  "Our privacy is breached. You're no longer a normal person. Our wedding was a circus. I think several people got arrested trying to climb up a mountain, trying to sneak in. We actually rented the airspace." She got married a third time to Marc Anthony in a private ceremony.

Jewelry

This year, Alex Rodriguez proposed to Jennifer Lopez and gave her a ring that costs at least $1 million.

J.Lo is a fan of jewelry. In 2013, she attended the Grammys and she wore $5 million in jewelry from Norman Silverman jewelry, including a diamond bracelet and diamond rings. It was impossible to look away.

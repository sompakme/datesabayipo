Craig Counsell Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Craig John Counsell (born August 21, 1970) is a retired Major League Baseball infielder from the United States. During his 15-year MLB career, he was a member of five different clubs. Counsell is best remembered for his postseason performances with the Florida Marlins in 1997 and the Arizona Diamondbacks in 2001. He was awarded the NLCS Most Valuable Player in 2001, and he has been on base for the past two World Series games that have concluded with a walk-off single. He was also renowned for his unusual hitting posture, in which he would raise his bat over his head and hold his bat in his hands.

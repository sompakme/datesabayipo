Wild woman!

It takes one bold woman to wear a dress like that on a date with her mama—but Hawn certainly didn’t seem to mind. On the contrary, the comedic actress, 69, clad in a Versace Resort cocktail dress and fur coat, cinched at the waist with a Medusa belt, loved her daughter’s bombshell moment so much, she even leaned in to squeeze Hudson’s pert tush for the paps.

What Is The Cast Of The Babysitter's Club Doing Now That The Show Is Over?

The Babysitters Club series on Netflix was based on the children's book series of the same name. It was a very well-done show and shared a modern-day retelling of some of the classic stories in the book series. Sadly, the series was canceled after only two seasons on the streaming platform.

The series starred a bunch of young up-and-coming actors as well as some acting veterans playing the parents, such as Alicia Silverstone and Mark Feuerstein. Many fans may be wondering what the cast is doing now that the series has ended. Well, one of the girls has gone on to star in Marvel and another has gone on to co-host a podcast. Let's see what the cast of Netflix's The Babysitter's Club is up to these days.

10 Alicia Silverstone Has Landed Several Roles Since The Babysitters Club

Alicia Silverstone has had the most onscreen roles since the cancelation of The Babysitters Club. She has appeared in films such as Last Survivors, The Requin, and Senior Year. She has also had guest appearances on television series such as Masters of the Universe: Revelation and American Horror Stories. She recently posted a video on her Instagram about how she misses being on set of The Babysitters Club with the cast. Fans miss the show, too!

9 Mark Feuerstein Has Guest-Starred On A Couple Of Series

Mark Feuerstein, who played Kristy's stepdad on the series, has had a recurring role on the television series Power Book II: Ghost. He also appeared in an episode of The Good Fight, titled "The End of Football." He is also credited with an appearance in a short film called The Homiesexuals: a social media tragedy.

8 Sophie Grace Is An Ambassador For The HALO Foundation

Sophie Grace, who played the role of Kristy on the series, hasn't landed any onscreen roles since the cancelation of The Babysitters Club, however, she is working as an ambassador for The HALO Foundation, which provides housing, healing, and education to at-risk and homeless youth.

7 Momona Tamada Made A Paramount+ Movie

Momona Tamada who played Claudia on the series had a role in a Paramount+ original movie titled Secret Headquarters, starring Owen Wilson. The film also stars actors Michael Pena and Jesse Williams. According to the film's synopsis, it tells the story of a boy named Charlie and his friends who end up discovering the headquarters of the world's most powerful superhero hidden beneath his home.

6 Shay Rudolph Co-Hosts A Podcast

Shay Rudolph, who played Stacey on the show, now co-hosts a podcast with Jess Weiner called The Smart Girl's Podcast. They talk to all kinds of celebrity guests including gymnast Nia Dennis and actress/musician McKenna Grace. In fact, Rudolph even starred in McKenna Grace's music video for her song "You Ruined Nirvana."

5 Malia Baker Starred In A Lifetime Movie

Malia Baker, who played the role of Mary Anne on The Babysitters Club, starred in a Lifetime movie in 2022 called Caught in His Web. She played the role of Olivia and is one of the main characters in the film.

4 Vivian Watson Is Enjoying Being A Teenager

Vivan Watson who played the role of Mallory on The Babysitters Club hasn't been doing much in the industry since the show was canceled. She seems to just be enjoying life as a teenager as she turned 14 at the end of 2021. Prior to The Babysitters Club, Watson only had two acting credits on IMDb; one for a short film called The Quaranteens and a voiceover role in two episodes of Chuggington. She is brand new to being in front of a camera, but hopefully, she will land some more work in the future!

3 Anais Lee Was In A Music Video

Anais Lee who played Jessi on The Babysitters Club has had a role in a music video by The Weeknd called "Die for You" and she has also guest-starred in a few episodes of the Netflix comedy series, The Upshaws. Lee is also a trained dancer who has trained with Debbie Allen. She's an incredibly talented young woman at the age of 15. She will definitely go far in the entertainment industry!

2 Xochiti Gomez Starred In A Marvel Movie

Xochiti Gomez wasn't able to play the role of Dawn during season two of the series, because she had landed the role of America Chavez in Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness. She told Collider that she received some great advice from Patrick Stewart who made an appearance in the film, and he reminded her that she should always treat people with kindness because you never know who the next big star might be.

1 Kyndra Sanchez Guest-Starred On Blue Bloods

Kyndra Sanchez, who replaced Gomez in the role of Dawn on The Babysitters Club, guest-starred in a season 12 episode of Blue Bloods on CBS called "Silver Linings." She portrayed a character named Soli Ruiz in an episode where two of the main characters search for an undocumented teenage girl who has been the victim of sex-trafficking.

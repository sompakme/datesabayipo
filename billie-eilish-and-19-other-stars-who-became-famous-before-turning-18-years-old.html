Billie Eilish And 19 Other Stars Who Became Famous Before Turning 18 Years Old

Billie Eilish is probably one of the biggest up and coming acts in the music industry, and she can easily become a massive success if she continues on the path that she is on. The singer had great triumph with her recent album and landed herself on the Hot 100 with a number one song.

Eilish is a prime example of young successes, and at only 17 years old, she is proving that anything can be done at any age. Along with Billie, there are countless other big names that made it big before the age of 18 and have gone on to become household names.

From Disney royalty, Netflix stars and singers alike, making it big before your 18 seems to be an industry pattern and one that definitely works! Without further ado, here is a list of stars like Billie Eilish that became famous before they were 18 years old.

40 Billie Eilish

39

Billie Eilish's first album 'When We Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?' was released this past March and was an instant success! The singer landed her first-ever number one song on the Hot 100 with 'Bad Guy' and went on to create a remix with Justin Bieber that also topped the charts!

38 Miley Cyrus

37

Miley Cyrus is one of the many members of Disney royalty that broke out on the scene far before she was 18. The star played the role of Disney's Hannah Montana and instantly became an overnight success! The singer is no stranger to the industry, considering she is the daughter of country star, Billy Ray Cyrus.

36 Millie Bobby Brown

35

Millie Bobby Brown is easily one of the most talked-about young starts today! The actress stars on Netflix's hit show "Stranger Things" as the telekinetic Eggo loving character, Eleven. The actress is only 15 years old and has amassed loads of success from this show and countless other projects the star is working on.

34 Justin Bieber

33

Justin Bieber broke out on the scene when he was only a YouTube star. Luckily for the Biebs, he was recognized by Usher and Scooter Braun who instantly made the singer into a  teen heartthrob. Bieber's first single "One Time" came out when he was just 14 years old and has since become one of the most successful singers of all time.

32 Normani

31

Normani first broke out on the scene when she auditioned for the X-Factor back in 2012 when she was only 16 years old. Although she didn't make it through as a solo act, she was put in the girl group, Fifth Harmony, that grew to become hugely popular. Normani has since gone on to a solo career and has had loads of success with her newest single "Motivation".

30 Shawn Mendes

29

Shawn Mendes is one of Canada's most notable singers who made it big before turning 18! Although Mendes first started off as a Vine star, he instantly became an overnight success with his song 'Stitches' back in 2015. The star is now 21 years old and is currently on a sold-out worldwide tour!

28 Camila Cabello

27

Speaking of Shawn Mendes, Camila Cabello is also one of the industries youngsters that made it big before 18. Cabello also started her career on 'The X-Factor', joining the girl group Fifth Harmony before making it big as a solo act with hit songs such as "Seniorita" and "Liar".

26 Lindsay Lohan

25

Lindsay Lohan is definitely one of the original Disney stars who gained international success as a child actress. The star was only 12 years old when 'The Parent Trap' was released. She later went on to star in other Disney films such as 'Freaky Friday' and 'Confessions Of A Teenage Drama Queen' all before turning 18.

24 Cole Sprouse

23

Cole Sprouse, who currently stars on the hit CW show 'Riverdale', made his debut with Disney alongside his twin brother Dylan Sprouse in 'Suite Life of Zack & Cody". Although this was his breakthrough role, Sprouse has been in the business since he was 7 years old starring in 'Big Daddy' and 'Friends' as Ben Geller.

22 Selena Gomez

21

Selena Gomez is yet another member of the Disney royal family. The 'Lose You To Love Me' singer was not only a star on Disney's 'Wizards of Waverly Place' when she was just a teenager, but she starred on "Barney & Friends" when she was only 7 years old. Gomez has gone on to become an international success and role model for millions of people.

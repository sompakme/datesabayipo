Knives Out is a brand new "who dunnit" flick directed by Rian Johnson. With a stellar cast, this movie follows a detective as he investigates the death of the patriarch of an eccentric and wild family, and no suspects are being ruled out. Of course, these types of films always stack the roster, so we're here to settle the score.

In case you're wondering which one of these brilliant actors or actresses has raked in the most dough, you no longer have to. Here are the top 10 cast members from the show and their net worth, ranked (according to Celebrity Net Worth).

Ana de Armas - $4 Million

Ana de Armas is only recently making waves in Hollywood, so we definitely expect her net worth to shoot through the roof in the next couple of years. You probably know this bombshell best from Blade Runner 2049, but she was also recently in War Dogs, The Informer, and Overdrive. Also, she has 5 movies in post-production or filming, so we know she isn't done. This talented and beautiful gal plays Marta Cabrera in this flick, and we know you loved her through every scene.

Katherine Langford - $5 Million

It's no secret that this young and rising actress got her breakout through her starring role as Hannah Baker on 13 Reasons Why. However, she's recently jumped over to film, and had a lead role in Love, Simon (2018).

She has another movie coming out soon, and is apparently part of a new television series, too. Of course, this young woman's net worth is definitely far from final, and we expect more incredible things from her.

Michael Shannon - $8 Million

This actor is definitely no newbie to the big screen. Nominated for 2 Oscars and with 95 acting credits under his belt, he's a veteran choice for Knives Out. This man's net worth comes from a ton of secondary roles in movies, including Man of Steel, Loving, The Shape of Water, Nocturnal Animals, and many more. Obviously, he brings some incredible talent to this flick, and we know he has some more projects coming soon.

Toni Collette - $18 Million

This Oscar-nominated actress is also one of our all-time favs. You probably know her best from About a Boy or Little Miss Sunshine, but most recently, this woman's net worth has been shooting up because of her role in Velvet Buzzsaw, Hereditary, and many more. She also has some more flicks in the works, so we're definitely excited to see more of this talented gal. Of course, she's also a pretty perfect Joni Thrombey in this crime masterpiece.

Christopher Plummer - $20 Million

We don't have to tell you that this veteran actor is a legend in the industry. With 1 Oscar to show for it, this man has over 200 acting credits. Clearly, this flick was not his first. Most recently, this man has done quite a bit of voice acting, but we know you recognize him (most recently) from All the Money in the World and Cliffs of Freedom. Honestly, we're surprised his net worth isn't higher, but clearly he's been in the business for a long time.

Don Johnson - $40 Million

This man also has almost 100 acting credits to show for his career. Most recently, you probably recognize him from Django Unchained, Dragged Across Concrete, and Book Club. Of course, his most iconic role is probably as the detective from Miami Vice in the '80s. Obviously, this man isn't done acting, and we love his versatility and talent. He was a perfect Richard Drysdale in this crime flick, and we're excited to see what else will contribute to this man's growing net worth.

Jamie Lee Curtis - $60 Million

We think everyone knows this brilliant and hilarious woman, right? Of course, we know her best from Freaky Friday and the Halloween series. Most recently, she actually returned for the newest flick from this franchise.

However, she's also dabbled in television, such as Scream Queens and New Girl. Of course, this net worth is not surprising, and this gal has four more flicks in the works (and yes, two of these are part of the Halloween franchise).

Chris Evans - $70 Million

It's pretty obvious that most of this whopping net worth is from this talented man's role as America's favorite ass (AKA Captain America). However, this man is also super versatile and brilliant, and has joined many different rom-coms, dramas, and action flicks. Most recently, you probably know him from Gifted, Before We Go, and The Red Sea Diving Resort. And, you know, all the Avengers stuff. While he's hanging up his infamous shield, we know this man isn't done showing us what he's made of, and this role was absolutely made for this brilliant man.

Frank Oz - $80 Million

This man is honestly a legend, and we know you probably recognize his voice more than his face. In fact, he almost exclusively does voice acting. That's right, this is the man behind Yoda, but he's also Grover and the Cookie Monster for all Sesame Street related projects. Obviously, it's not surprising that his net worth is what it is. Still, director Rian Johnson decided to let this man finally show his face, and he plays Alan Stevens in this crime flick. However, he'll always be Yoda in our hearts.

Daniel Craig - $130 Million

It's also probably not surprising to see this man at the top of the list. Obviously, being in a bunch of James Bond flicks as 007 himself really racks up your net worth. Of course, he's been in a handful of Bond films, including Skyfall and Spectre, and will be appearing again in the upcoming No Time to Die. However, he's also been in many other dramas, and even has one in production now. He's certainly made a name for himself in Hollywood, and we know he isn't done yet. This legendary actor was the perfect detective as Benoit Blanc in this incredible "who dunnit" crime mystery.

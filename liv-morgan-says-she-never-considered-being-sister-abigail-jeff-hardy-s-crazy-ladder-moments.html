Liv Morgan Says She Never Considered Being Sister Abigail

WWE fans remember a year ago when Liv Morgan was the outright front runner to be the Sister Abigail character. Liv Morgan was taking a sabbatical from WWE last year and consistently sent out cryptic dark themed tweets that led many fans to believe it was all leading up for her to be revealed as Sister Abigail.

Sportskeeda recently caught up with Liv Morgan and asked if these rumors had any truth to them. Liv said, “I think it’s very flattering fanfiction. I, I was never, you know, respectfully, hoping to be Sister Abigail. I was never pushing to be Sister Abigail. I went away for a while and I’m finding myself and I’m exploring myself and I have all these thoughts and emotions and I’m trying to let people know without literally saying it. And they just, kind of , wanted me to return so bad. I think they felt that was it. That was what I was going to be doing. That’s a very flattering, you know, fanfic. But all it is is fanfic.”

Fans now seem to think that Alexa Bliss is in line to become Sister Abigail, and only time will tell if that is the direction that creative goes.

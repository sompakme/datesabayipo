Who Is Jason Scott Lee? Everything About The American Actor And Martial Artist

Jason Scott Lee is a popular Actor and Martial Artist; people are curious about his kids and parents. Read more to find out.

Jason Scott Lee is an American actor and martial artist.

He is best known for performing Bruce Lee in the martial arts film Dragon: The Bruce Lee Story in 1993.

Moreover, his character Mowgli is also popular in Disney’s live-action adaption of The Jungle Book in 1994.

Lee’s acting career began with small roles in films such as Born in East L.A. (1987) and Back to the Future Part II (1989).

In the 2000 movie Arabian Nights, he played Aladdin. In addition, he did voice-over work for the Disney animated feature Lilo & Stitch, which was released in 2002.

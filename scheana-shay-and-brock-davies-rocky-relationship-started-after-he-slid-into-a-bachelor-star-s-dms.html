Scheana Shay And Brock Davies' Rocky Relationship Started After He Slid Into A Bachelor Stars DMs

Highlights

  • Brock Davies and Scheana Shay's relationship faced issues, including communication problems and Brock's DMs to Bachelor star Shanae Ankney.
  • Scheana admitted she didn't see a future with Brock, but they worked on their relationship through therapy and brain chemistry alignment.
  • The couple is now in a better place, focusing on their love and respect for each other and the well-being of their daughter, Summer.

Vanderpump Rules stars' Brock Davies and Scheana Shay have had their share of rocky patches in their relationship. At the end of the day, poor choices can be a bone of contention. One such choice occurred when Brock messaged a Bachelor star when he was engaged to Sheana.

Unlike Adriana Madox and Tom Sandoval who ended their relationship filled with a dramatic scandal after Tom cheated, sparking rumors that Scandoval was for ratings purposes, Brock and Scheana are not ready to end their marriage. But, there are still clearly issues that need to be worked on. As such, we will look at Brock Davies and Scheana Shay's rocky relationship that started after he slid into a Bachelor star's DM. Then why Scheana told Brock she did not see them together forever will be discussed, followed by where Scheana and Brock's relationship stands today.

Brock Davies Slid Into The Bachelor's Shanae Ankney's DMs During A Rocky Period With Scheana Shay

Brock and Scheana met in 2019 and the two jumped into a relationship very quickly. They lived through the pandemic together, suffered through a miscarriage six months after becoming a couple, and by 2021, had a daughter together, dealt with Brock abandoning his kids with his ex-wife to pursue his career, and were engaged.

While the couple claimed that the experiences made them closer, they also admitted to dealing with issues in their relationship. During a period when the couple was arguing over how to raise their daughter, Summer, they were constantly arguing. It was in this timeframe that Brock DM'd Bachelor star, Shanae Ankney.

Close

Scheana admitted during a February 2022 episode of Scheananagins with Scheana, that Brock reached out to Shanae with a simple comment.

"You're making great TV."

Shanae responded to Brock and thanked him for the compliment. But, as Scheana explained, the conversation continued between the two. This was because, according to Scheana, Brock wanted Shanae to be part of his Homebody app he was trying to launch at the time.

While Scheana said that she was okay with Brock reaching out to Shanae, fans were not so convinced. Many wondered if Scheana was just jumping out before the story could become a scandal, especially with Brock having a history of flirting and texting other women when he was with his ex-wife.

Others still questioned why Scheana and Brock's PR team was not reaching out to Shanae rather than Brock DMing her.

It was not until April 2022, that Scheana would admit that she and Brock were having problems on Scheananagins with Scheana. The problems, Scheana said, had to do with how to parent Summer. This was specifically dealing with "parenting and, like, sleep training and the things she eats."

"It’s affecting our relationship. It’s affecting our sleep, which affects our mood."

It is unclear if Brock was messaging Shanae during one of their arguments about Summer. But, the timeline does not bode well for a happy relationship when in the throes of trying to parent when not on the same page about how to raise a child.

Scheana Shay Told Brock Davies She Didn't See Them Together Forever

Despite getting individual therapy and trying couple's therapy, Scheana and Brock were still having problems with their relationship in 2023-2024. This, again, mainly had to do with the way Summer was being parented. It also had to do with the fact that Scheana was learning to deal with her postpartum obsessive-compulsive disorder. This made it so that Scheana would picture the worst of things happening to Summer when she was not in her care.

Because Scheana was always afraid something was going to happen to Summer, it made it so that she did not want to leave the house without her. As such, Scheana and Brock got very little time away from their daughter. This was frustrating for Brock, especially since they did have trusted people to watch Summer.

Close

One of the times that Brock and Scheana did step out for a night out was when they went to dinner for their one-year Cancun, Mexico wedding anniversary. It was during this time that Scheana dropped a bombshell on Brock that brought him to tears.

"I don't think you and I are going to be together forever."

While Scheana did go on to explain that she did not want to have thoughts like that, the way that the two were communicating with one another was taking a toll on their relationship. Scheana wanted to work on things and get their relationship on track, but needed Brock to meet her halfway.

Scheana and Brock were able to get past their emotional conversation at dinner and appeared to be on the same page with their relationship. But even after laying all of their thoughts and fears out for one another to see, rumors still swirled that the relationship between Scheana and Brock was on thin ice.

Where Scheana Shay And Brock Davies' Relationship Stands Today

There is no denying that Scheana and Brock have been through quite a bit since getting together in 2019. But, this past year has been one of the most difficult for the two, as was seen during Season 11 of Vanderpump Rules. While the two put on a good front for their friends and in front of the camera at times, there were problems bubbling below the surface and even momentary questioning if Brock and Raquel Leviss had anything going on in the wake of Scandoval.

Fortunately, according to Scheana, during an April 2024, Scheananagins with Scheana, the two are in a "much better place now."

Close

Scheana admitted that she and Brock had gone through "a lot of issues" in 2023, which played out for audiences in 2024. While it was a "difficult time" in their relationship, Scheana feels like they have made it through a lot of the problems plaguing their marriage.

"I think that after everything we went through and have been in therapy about individually, I think we're in the best place we've ever been in."

Scheana noted that every relationship has its ups and downs. But it was through communicating with one another that Scheana and Brock were able to remember how much "love" and "respect" they had for one another and how much they wanted their marriage to work.

"We want our family to work."

As much as the couple thought that couple's therapy would be the answer for them, they were not able to find that it worked for them. Instead, they turned to getting their brain chemistry aligned which, as Scheana shared on the Jeff Lewis Has Issues podcast, was a game changer for the couple.

After getting their brain chemistry aligned via a supplement program prescribed by their psychiatrist and brain disorder specialist, Scheana and Brock were able to better communicate. This, and continuing their respective individual therapies, according to Scheana, has helped save their marriage.

What the future holds for Brock and Scheana is not certain. But what is clear is that the two love one another and want one another to have the best lives and give the best life to their daughter, Summer. While there are not any plans or pressure to have any other children soon, the couple is not closing the doors on expanding their family. For now, though, the family of three is content in the life they have built for themselves, that only appears to be getting stronger as time goes on.

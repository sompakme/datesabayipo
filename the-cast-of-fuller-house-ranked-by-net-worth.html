The Cast Of 'Fuller House,' Ranked By Net Worth

One, big, happy family! Full House was an American sitcom that aired on ABC from 1987 to 1995. The show chronicles the events of widowed father Danny Tanner who enlists his brother-in-law, Jesse Katsopolis, and best friend, Joey Gladstone to help raise his three daughters, D.J., Stephanie and Michelle.

That show jump started the careers of many of the actors and has become a cult classic, but many of them are still known for their iconic roles, over 25 years later. Full House was so popular that it sparked a spin-off show, Fuller House- which centers around two of the daughters-D.J. and Stephanie, their friend Kimmy (as adults) and their kids' lives. The Olsen twins did not return for the reboot, as they quit acting in 2011. And the men of Full House had recurring roles on Fuller House.

Between scandals and deaths of certain cast members, many net worths have changed dramatically. Here is the cast of Fuller House, ranked by net worth.

9 Soni Nicole Bringas' Net Worth Is Between $500,000 to $800,000

Soni Nicole Bringas played Ramona Gibbler, Kimmy's teenage daughter. The 19-year-old has also appeared in Jane the Virgin, Instant Mom, Beautiful and Twisted, Minutiae and more, including commercial work, according to IMDb. She is also a dancer and has worked on Dancing With The Stars and with famous artists, such as Pitbull and Justin Bieber. Her net worth is not confirmed, but according to a few websites it is estimated to be around $500,000 to $800,000.

8 Michael Campion's Net Worth Is About $800,000

Michael Campion played Jackson Fuller, D.J. Tanner's mischievous teenage son. According to IMDb, Campion has starred in the films Robo-Dog and Christmas Trade. As for TV, he acted in Red Ruby and The Final Exam, as well as commercials. Besides on-screen acting, the 19-year-old has also dabbled in stage acting such as The Little Mermaid, Wizard of Oz and Annie. His net worth is unclear, but some websites peg him to be worth around $800,000.

7 Andrea Barber's Net Worth Is $2 Million

Andrea Barber played Kimmy Gibbler, D.J. Tanner's best friend and became a recurring character on the show, who eventually got one of the lead roles in Fuller House. After the show, she retired from acting to focus on her personal life but came back for the reboot. She has starred in other shows such as Days of Our Lives. She recently starred in the 2021 Nickelodeon show, That Girl Lay Lay. From her time acting, Barber has amassed a net worth of $2 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

6 Jodie Sweetin's Net Worth Is $2 Million

Jodie Sweetin played Stephanie Tanner, the middle child. Despite Sweetin acting in many other roles besides the Full House franchise, her net worth is only $2 million as of this year. She earned most of her money from acting and her autobiography, "UnSweetin," in which she talked about her drug abuse, which caused her to take a break from the entertainment industry.

5 Dave Coulier's Net Worth Is $5 Million

Dave Coulier played Danny Tanner's best friend, Joey. He was a regular on the show. Besides earning his money mostly from Full House, Coulier continued to act in many other TV shows and a few not as popular movies. Those roles, plus his career in stand-up comedy has earned him a net worth of $5 Million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He tours the U.S. and Canada as a stand-up comedian.

4 Candace Cameron Bure's Net Worth Is $14 Million

Candace Cameron Bure played D.J. Tanner, the oldest of the daughters. Besides earning her $14 million net worth from acting in both Full House series, she is also the star of numerous Hallmark Christmas movies, as well as Make It Or Break It and many other made for TV films. Writing four books also contributed to her fortune as well as brand deals.

3 John Stamos' Net Worth Is $25 Million

John Stamos, played Uncle Jesse, everyone's favorite uncle and brother of Danny Tanner. Stamos has a pretty impressive filmography to go along with his impressive net worth of $25 million. The Emmy-nominated actor went on to star in roles such as ER, General Hospital, Big Shot, Grandfathered, Scream Queens, My Big Fat Greek Wedding and more. Besides acting, he has earned his money through theater productions and releasing music.

2 Bob Saget's Net Worth Was $50 Million

At the time of his death in January 2022, Bob Saget was worth $50 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. His career expanded way beyond Full House, where he played Danny Tanner. Saget was the original host of America's Funniest Home Videos for many years, as well as the game show, 1 vs. 100. He was the narrator for How I Met Your Mother for all nine seasons. The Grammy-nominated actor starred in many TV shows and movies, including Raising Dad, Surviving Suburbia, The Masked Singer and more.

However, his one true love was stand-up. Just before his death, Saget was on tour for the first time in many years performing a new stand-up show, and he also started a podcast in 2020. All of these contributed to his massive net worth.

1 Lori Loughlin's Net Worth Is $70 Million

Lori Loughlin portrayed Aunt Becky on Full House. She was a part of the reboot but did not appear in the last season due to the college admissions scandal. Despite the court dates, her time in prison and having payback the tuition for her daughter's school, she still amassed a pretty hefty net worth of $70 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. That is her combined net worth with her husband. She did go on to act in other roles besides Full House including 90210, In Case of Emergency and many Hallmark Christmas movies. Loughlin has also earned her money through producing and modeling.

Donald Clarence “Don” Simpson (October 29, 1943 – January 19, 1996) was an American film producer, screenwriter, and actor who worked in the film industry for almost three decades. Simpson, together with his producing partner Jerry Bruckheimer, was responsible for such blockbuster pictures as Flashdance (1983), Beverly Hills Cop (1984), Top Gun (1986), and The Rock (1988), among others (1996). Their flicks would go on to gross a total of $3 billion across the globe.

Billie Eilish &amp; Other Celebs Who Rock Acrylic Nails

Acrylic nails aren’t for everyone but those who choose to rock them usually look amazing! There is something so stylish and trendy about acrylic nails because they have the potential to come in so many different colors with so many different designs. Depending on the person wearing them, acrylic nails can be extremely long or they can be shorter to appear more natural.

RELATED: Everything The Kardashian-Jenner Women Have Said About Fashion

There are so many fun things that a person can do when they go to the nail salon and their options are wind up in a big way when they decide to get acrylic nails rather than normal manicures on their natural nails. These celebrities know that acrylic nails look amazing on them so even if they don’t wear acrylic nails all the time, they have still been spotted wearing them often enough.

10 Lana Del Rey

Lana Del Rey with acrylic nails is such a look. In a lot of her music videos, she is seen wearing acrylic nails! For example, in her music video for “Burning Desire“ she is on stage in front of a red curtain holding onto a microphone and singing the sultry lyrics at a very low level. As she holds onto the microphone, she is gripping it with her bright red acrylic nails! Something about the entire look just screams “passion.”

9 Rihanna

Rihanna has been known to wear acrylic nails pretty often. The coolest thing about Rihanna’s acrylic nails is the fact that they seem to go along flawlessly with other accessories she wears such as rings on almost every finger. She also has a lot of tattoos on her hands so seeing the combination of hard-core tattoos with super feminine acrylic nails is a nice balance.

8 Kylie Jenner

It is obvious that Kylie Jenner is the acrylic nail princess. She has been getting her acrylic nails done regularly for years and she always posts whatever her new set looks like on Instagram and Snapchat. Interestingly enough, diehard Kylie Jenner fans like to keep up with their own manicure is based on whatever picture Kylie Jenner posts. Her acrylic nails are usually always long but she always changes the colors up.

7 Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish loves to rock acrylic nails, especially when she is on stage and walking red carpets. Her acrylic nails are not meant to look realistic or natural. She gets them extremely long! Billie Eilish with her acrylic nails is a whole entire vibe. Whether she is rocking acrylic nails that are a basic black color or she’s making a statement with neon green (like her hair) she always knows how to make sure it looks good.

6 Khloe Kardashian

Sisters can be so similar! That seems to be the case for Khloe Kardashian and her younger sister Kylie Jenner. Out of the entire Kardashian clan, these two are the main ones who regularly wear acrylic nails.

RELATED: Khloe Kardashian's Latest Post Has Fans Confusing Her With Kendall Jenner

Khloe Kardashian wears her acrylic nails just as long as Kylie Jenner does and she also constantly changes up the color. She keeps it simple without adding designs or crystals to her nails for a classier look.

5 Gwen Stefani

Gwen Stefani is widely recognized for her bleach blonde hair, washboard abs, and bright red lipstick. Something else that she often also does? She wears acrylic nails! Gwen Stefani has been active in the music scene since the late 80s And she is still just as relevant today as she was back then. She knows how to keep up with the trends… Including when it comes to her acrylic nails.

4 Fergie

Imagine running into Fergie at the nail salon! That would be really interesting and a lot of fun since she seems to have a great personality. And something else she has is a great sense of style. Her taste (especially with her manicures) is very much on point.

RELATED: Where Is The Black Eyed Peas' Fergie Now?

Pictured here, she has seen wearing a metallic shade of gray on her nails in both pictures. In one picture, she’s on a red carpet, and in the other, she is posing for a photoshoot. Either way, the metallic gray looks futuristic.

3 Zendaya

Zendaya has been wearing acrylic nails for quite some time! When she was on the Disney Channel starring in KC Undercover, she was even wearing acrylic nails back then. They are definitely part of her standard look and she knows how to rock them in a majorly classy way. She wears simple colors that look great with her skin tone. She keeps up with her manicures as much as she keeps up with everything else.

2 Lady Gaga

Acrylic nails go hand-in-hand with Lady Gaga's style. Her style is oftentimes very wild, crazy, and eccentric. Sometimes the things she wears totally jump out at you! Who remembers the time she wore a dress made of me?! Her nails are usually always a bit edgier and crazier than the average. It would be rarer to see Lady Gaga with a simple nail color rather than a crazy nail design.

1 Dua Lipa

Dua Lipa has posted some of her acrylic nail sets on social media for her fans to admire because every time she gets acrylic nails done, she doesn’t go easy with it. She goes over the top and it is always perfect! Dua Lipa is the type of musician who stands out in a crowd and she makes sure that her nails are adding to her overall effect.

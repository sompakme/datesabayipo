Katy Perry Parades Her Trim Bikini Body in Retro Swimsuit: Photo

No sympathy for your fans? 

As cold temps settle on much of the country, Katy Perry teased us all via pictures from a private yacht called "Sassy" in Australia on Monday, Nov. 17.

Not only is she in a warmer climate, but the "Roar" singer has been putting in work at the gym, as proven by her enviable abs and toned legs in the picture. 

"G2G my whip is here," the singer wrote in the photo caption. 

Currently down under on her Prismatic World Tour, Perry, 30, served up fitness inspiration in a baby blue retro-style bathing suit, paired with a sun-shielding bucket hat, a matching beach bag, and dark sunglasses.

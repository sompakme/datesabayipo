North West, Blue Ivy Carter, And Other Celebrity Kids Who Will Rule Hollywood

Celebrity children are immune to the spotlight. They are born into a lifestyle where the paparazzi follows them around, they make red carpet appearances, and get a helping hand to get their start in the industry.

Meet the kids that may one day rule Hollywood.

Emme Maribel Muñiz

Jennifer Lopez & Marc Anthony’s daughter, Emme is no stranger to the spotlight. Ever since Emme debuted her rendition of Alicia Key’s hit song “If I Ain’t Got You,” the internet has been raving about her stellar voice.

The 11-year-old joined her mom on stage during the It’s My Party tour to perform Jennifer’s 2018 song “Limitless” and also appeared in the song’s music video. Emme also joined her mom on the world's biggest stage during the 2020 Super Bowl.

The Super Bowl Halftime Show at 11-years-old? Without a doubt, Emme Muñiz will be taking over the music industry.

Blue Ivy Carter

Blue Ivy Carter was a protégé in the making since her birth. The oldest child of Beyoncé and Jay Z has been breaking records since she was born. In a matter of days since her birth, Blue Ivy became the youngest person ever to appear on a Billboard chart, thanks to her cameo on Jay-Z's fatherhood track, “Glory (feat. B.I.C.),” which was released two days after her arrival. The track features audio of then baby Blue Ivy’s cries and other baby sounds. The song debuted at No. 74 on Billboard’s R&B/Hip Hop Songs chart.

That wasn’t Blue Ivy’s only record. Last Summer at just 7-years-old, Blue Ivy was featured on her mom’s track “Brown Skin Girl” for The Lion King’s soundtrack. Not only does Blue Ivy sing on the song, but the Carter’s first-born was also given a writing credit and a composing credit, making her first appearance into songwriting. Something tells us Blue Ivy will be making more appearances on the Billboard charts in the near future.

Moroccan & Monroe Cannon

Moroccan & Monroe Cannon are the children of best-selling female artist Mariah Carey and TV host Nick Cannon. The 8-year-old twins already have experience performing live having opened for their mom’s “All I Want For Christmas Is You” Las Vegas residency.

They also made cameo appearances for the 2019 version of their mom’s #1 hit of the same name. Whether they decide to continue the twin duo or go solo, the Cannon Twins are sure to be entertaining us in music, television or both.

North West

North West, daughter of Kanye West and Kim Kardashian, seems to be following in her dad’s footsteps with her love of music. The six-year-old has shared the stage with her father Kanye during several of his Sunday Service gatherings. In a video posted by her mom, North is singing along to the music, which captured her father’s attention. Kanye brought the mic to North’s mouth and she sang along.

This isn’t the first time North has stolen the show. During another Sunday Services gathering, North passionately sang along to the music while Kanye and the choir grooved in the background. Kanye, a 21-time Grammy winner, looked like a proud parent with a giant grin on his face throughout the clip.
